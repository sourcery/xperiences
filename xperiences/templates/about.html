A shared meal is one of those experiences that defines what it means to be human.
Eating together with family, friends and neighbors strengthens community, bridges cultures, and builds a robust local economy.
We know that there are culinary wonders going on behind countless closed doors. Pies cooling on the windowsills, fresh cooking smells wafting by the noses of wistful passerbys. We unite them on the web for the first time. We want to encourage more people to sit down to the table with strangers and get up as friends. We want to encourage [amateur and aspiring] gourmands to share their favorite dish with travelers and teach their neighbors the secret recipe for the perfect loaf of bread.
